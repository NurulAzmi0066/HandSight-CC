Features

Login

With the Intuitive Login Feature, users are invited to enter a world of easier access. HandSight ensures there are no boundaries for anyone.

Camera Translate

With the HandSight Translate Camera Feature, users can point the app's camera at sign language gestures. The app identifies the gesture and converts it into text that is read on the screen.

History

Save previous recordings of Sign Language translation. Every time the user uses the translation feature, a recording of the translated text is automatically saved in the history.
